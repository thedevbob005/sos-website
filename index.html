About Simplified Office Services

Professional expertise with over 2 decades of experience

Our team members have certifications in:

  • Chartered Accountancy (Equivalent to CPA / ACCA)
  • CFA, US (ICFA)– Level 2 Complete
  • M.Sc Finance
  • Masters in Business Administration – (Banking & Finance)

Headquartered in India, SOS is a team of professionals having hands-on fulltime work experience of more than 2 decades in the field of Accounts, Audit, Banking and Finance. Our expertise spans across comprehensive financial consulting, detailed accounting services, and strategic business analysis.

We specialize in delivering high-quality financial modeling, equity valuation reports, and professional presentations that meet international standards. Whether you're a startup or an established company, we provide tailored solutions that align with your specific business needs.
